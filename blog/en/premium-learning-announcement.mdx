When we launched AI Educademy, we made a promise: high-quality AI education should be accessible to everyone, regardless of where they live or what they can afford. That promise hasn't changed. What has changed is how we sustain and grow the platform to serve our community better.

Today, we're introducing **AI Educademy Pro** — a premium tier that unlocks the full depth of our content, tools, and features — alongside a permanent commitment to keeping beginner content free. Forever.

This post explains exactly what's changing, what stays free, and why we believe this is the right path forward.

---

## What Stays Free — Forever

Let's start with the most important part. The following programs are and will always be **completely free**, with no sign-up walls, no trial expirations, and no feature gating:

- **AI Seeds** — Your first introduction to artificial intelligence
- **AI Sprouts** — Building foundational AI literacy
- **AI Sketch** — Understanding AI concepts visually and intuitively
- **AI Launchpad** — Building your AI career portfolio from scratch

These four programs represent the on-ramp to AI education. They cover the fundamentals that everyone needs — whether you're a student, a career switcher, a curious professional, or someone who just wants to understand what's happening in the world. Restricting access to foundational knowledge was never on the table and never will be.

Additionally, **the first lesson of every program on the platform is free to preview**. Even for Pro-only programs, you can read the opening lesson, see the teaching style, and judge the quality before committing a single penny. No bait-and-switch.

## What Pro Unlocks

AI Educademy Pro is for learners who want to go deeper — into advanced topics, specialised skills, career preparation, and hands-on practice. Here's what a Pro subscription gives you:

### Full Access to All 15 Programs and 100+ Lessons

Pro unlocks every program across all three learning tracks — AI Learning, Craft Engineering, and the newly launched Career Ready track. That's 15 programs and over 100 expert-crafted lessons covering everything from machine learning fundamentals to ML system design to interview preparation.

Every lesson is available in all 11 supported languages, and new content is added regularly. Your subscription grows with the platform.

### AI Mock Interview Lab

Our new AI-powered mock interview simulator gives you unlimited practice sessions across behavioural, technical, and ML-specific interview formats. Each session provides detailed, actionable feedback on your answers — what you did well, what needs improvement, and specific suggestions for next time.

It's the kind of deliberate practice that used to require hiring a career coach. Now it's available on demand, as many times as you need it.

### Completion Certificates

For every program you complete, you'll receive a verifiable completion certificate that you can add to your LinkedIn profile, include in job applications, or share with your employer. Certificates include the program name, completion date, and a unique verification link.

We know certificates alone don't prove competence — but they do signal commitment, and in a competitive job market, every signal matters.

## Pricing That Respects Your Budget

We've structured our pricing around one principle: the longer your commitment, the more you save. No hidden fees, no auto-renewal surprises, and you can cancel anytime.

### Monthly Plan
Full Pro access, billed monthly. Flexible and low-commitment — perfect if you want to try it for a month or focus on a specific program.

### Annual Plan — Save 34%
Everything in Monthly, billed annually at a significant discount. This is our most popular option and the best value for learners working through multiple programs over time.

### Lifetime Plan
One payment, permanent access. Every current program, every future program, every feature — forever. This is for learners who are serious about building long-term AI skills and want to lock in access without thinking about renewals.

## Why We're Charging — And Why We're Transparent About It

Building a platform like AI Educademy isn't free. We want to be direct about what your subscription supports:

**Content creation.** Every lesson is written, reviewed, and refined by professionals with real-world AI experience. Quality educational content takes significant time and expertise to produce.

**AI infrastructure.** Features like the Mock Interview Lab rely on large language model APIs that have real per-usage costs. Every practice session, every piece of feedback, every simulated interview — those API calls cost money, and we absorb that cost so you don't have to worry about token limits or pay-per-use pricing.

**Translation and localisation.** We don't run our content through a machine translator and call it done. Our 11-language support involves professional translators who understand both the language and the technical subject matter. That's an ongoing investment we're committed to.

**Platform development.** New features, performance improvements, accessibility enhancements, mobile optimisation — the engineering work never stops, and we want to keep improving the experience for everyone.

**Keeping beginner content free.** This is the part people don't always think about. Maintaining free content for tens of thousands of learners requires infrastructure, bandwidth, and support. Pro subscriptions directly subsidise free access for others.

We're not a venture-backed startup optimising for growth metrics. We're building a sustainable platform that can serve the AI education community for years to come. Charging fairly for advanced content is how we do that.

## What This Means for Existing Users

If you've been using AI Educademy's free content, **nothing changes for you**. Your access to AI Seeds, AI Sprouts, AI Sketch, and AI Launchpad remains exactly as it is. No features have been removed, no content has been paywalled that was previously free.

Pro is purely additive. It's new content, new tools, and new features — layered on top of an existing free experience that we're committed to maintaining and improving.
