# AI in Everyday Life - From Shopping to Space

You might think AI is something that lives in research labs and sci-fi films. In reality, you've probably already used AI a dozen times today - before you even had lunch.

## 🛒 Shopping and E-Commerce

Ever noticed how online shops seem to *know* what you want? That's AI at work:

- **Recommendation engines** - "Customers who bought this also bought…" is powered by AI analysing millions of purchase patterns
- **Dynamic pricing** - flight and hotel prices change in real time based on demand, time of day, and your browsing history
- **Visual search** - snap a photo of a pair of trainers and AI can find where to buy them online
- **Chatbots** - those helpful (and sometimes less helpful) chat windows use natural language processing to answer your questions

<FunFact>Amazon's recommendation engine generates roughly 35% of its total revenue. That "you might also like" section is one of the most profitable AI systems ever built.</FunFact>

## ✈️ Travel and Transport

AI is your invisible travel companion:

- **Route optimisation** - Google Maps and Waze use AI to predict traffic and find faster routes in real time
- **Ride-hailing** - Uber and Bolt use AI to match you with drivers, calculate fares, and predict demand
- **Airline operations** - AI helps airlines optimise fuel consumption, schedule crews, and predict maintenance needs
- **Autonomous vehicles** - self-driving cars use AI to interpret camera feeds, lidar data, and road conditions

## 🎬 Entertainment

The shows you watch, the songs you hear, and the posts you scroll through are all curated by AI:

- **Netflix** - its recommendation system saves the company an estimated $1 billion per year by keeping subscribers engaged
- **Spotify** - Discover Weekly analyses your listening habits alongside millions of other users to suggest new music you'll likely enjoy
- **TikTok** - its "For You" feed uses one of the most sophisticated recommendation algorithms ever created, learning your preferences from every swipe and pause
- **Gaming** - AI controls non-player characters, adjusts difficulty, and even generates entire game worlds

<ThinkAboutIt>Think about your social media feed. How much of what you see is chosen by AI versus what you actively searched for? Does that change how you think about the content you consume?</ThinkAboutIt>

## 🏠 Smart Homes

Modern homes are increasingly AI-powered:

- **Voice assistants** - Alexa, Siri, and Google Assistant use speech recognition and natural language processing
- **Smart thermostats** - devices like Nest learn your schedule and preferences to optimise heating
- **Robot vacuums** - they map your home and plan efficient cleaning routes
- **Security cameras** - AI distinguishes between a person at the door and a cat crossing the garden

## 🏥 Healthcare

AI is quietly revolutionising medicine:

- **Medical imaging** - AI can detect signs of breast cancer, lung disease, and diabetic eye disease in scans, sometimes earlier than human specialists
- **Drug discovery** - AI models can predict which molecules might work as medicines, slashing years off the research process
- **Mental health** - AI-powered apps provide cognitive behavioural therapy exercises and mood tracking
- **Epidemic prediction** - AI systems monitor global data to spot disease outbreaks early

## 🌾 Agriculture and Weather

AI helps feed the world and predict the weather:

- **Precision farming** - drones with AI analyse crop health, identify pests, and recommend exactly where to apply fertiliser
- **Weather forecasting** - Google DeepMind's GraphCast model can predict weather up to 10 days ahead more accurately than traditional methods
- **Yield prediction** - farmers use AI to estimate harvests and plan logistics

## 🚀 Space Exploration

Even beyond our planet, AI plays a vital role:

- **Mars rovers** - NASA's Perseverance rover uses AI to autonomously navigate rocky terrain and select interesting rocks to study
- **Satellite imagery** - AI processes millions of satellite photos to monitor deforestation, urban growth, and natural disasters
- **Exoplanet discovery** - AI has helped identify thousands of planets outside our solar system by analysing telescope data

<FunFact>NASA's AI system helped discover an eighth planet in the Kepler-90 star system in 2017, making it the first known star system (besides our own) with eight planets.</FunFact>

## 🎯 The Big Picture

AI isn't some distant technology waiting to arrive. It's already here - in your pocket, your home, your doctor's office, and orbiting above your head. Understanding where AI operates helps you become a more informed consumer, citizen, and thinker.
